What we do

CRM Operations is responsible for the end-to-end execution of all CRM campaigns across Sportsbook, Casino, Predicts, and Flywheel. Our scope covers campaign build, QA, personalisation, audience segmentation, and deployment across email, push, and in-app channels.

We manage the full campaign pipeline — from brief intake and AI-assisted triage through to final QA and live deployment — using Braze, Xtremepush, Hightouch, and Movable Ink. The team is actively embedding AI and automation into BAU workflows to raise quality, increase capacity, and reduce manual friction.

2026 Workstreams

Key Initiatives

Active and upcoming workstreams across AI automation, infrastructure, governance, and innovation. Updated quarterly.

Active workstreams
1
Braze → Xtremepush Migration
Full migration of CRM campaign delivery infrastructure from Braze to Xtremepush. Covers journey mapping, template migration, UAT, and go-live sequencing across all products. Zero disruption to live campaign SLAs is the primary constraint.
Active — In Progress
2
AI-Embedded Campaign Build & QA
Embedding AI assistance into the campaign build and QA workflow to reduce manual checks, catch errors earlier, and increase throughput. Includes prompt frameworks for copywriting, audience brief parsing, and automated QA logic in Xtremepush.
Active — Piloting
3
Hightouch Audience Orchestration
Establishing Hightouch as the central audience-building and syncing layer, connecting data from Databricks and Redshift into Xtremepush and Braze. Reduces reliance on ad hoc audience requests and enables self-serve segmentation.
Active — Building
4
CRM Governance & SLA Framework
Designing and rolling out standardised intake SLAs, campaign brief templates, and capacity visibility tooling. Includes a live dashboard showing queue health, team bandwidth, and in-flight campaign status across products.
Planned — Q2 2026
5
Tentpole Campaign Innovation Programme
Building a repeatable playbook for delivering standout CRM experiences at major sporting and gaming events — combining personalisation, real-time triggers, and Movable Ink dynamic content. First activation: 2026 NFL Season Opener.
Planned — Q3 2026
6
Personalisation at Scale (Movable Ink)
Expanding the use of Movable Ink for real-time, data-driven personalisation in email and push. Focus on live odds, tailored offers, and behavioural triggers across SBK and CAS products.
